About the Breed

🏔️ Origin

Bernese Mountain Dogs originate from the Swiss Alps, where they were bred as working dogs for farmers. Known for their strength, intelligence, and loyalty.

❤️ Temperament

Affectionate, loyal, and gentle. These dogs are excellent family companions and are known for their patient nature with children and other pets.

💪 Characteristics

Large, sturdy dogs with distinctive black, white, and rust coloring. They're intelligent, trainable, and thrive with an active lifestyle.

🏃 Activity Level

Moderate to high energy. They enjoy outdoor activities, hiking, and playtime. Regular exercise keeps them healthy and happy.

Breed Standards

Size:

Males: 25-27.5 inches | Females: 23-26 inches

Weight:

Males: 80-115 lbs | Females: 70-95 lbs

Lifespan:

7-10 years on average

Coat:

Double coat, black with rust and white markings

Health & Care Guidelines

🏥 Regular Vet Checkups

Schedule annual veterinary visits. Monitor for hip dysplasia and elbow dysplasia, common in the breed.

🪮 Grooming

Brush their double coat 2-3 times per week. They shed seasonally, so regular grooming is essential.

🥗 Nutrition

Feed high-quality dog food appropriate for large breeds. Maintain healthy weight to reduce joint stress.

💨 Exercise

Provide daily exercise and mental stimulation. They thrive with outdoor activities and training.
